WORD FREQUENCIES

Word frequency is the count/frequency of each word that appear in a source of textual data. A word cloud is the most probable way to visualize those frequencies. It is a collection, or cluster, of words with the size and/or color of each word indicating its relative frequency or importance. So, the more often a specific words appears in the text, the bigger bolder it appears in the word cloud and can help to identify the overused words. A word cloud can also show which topics are being discussed. Further it will highlight the jargon and vocabulary which is used.
